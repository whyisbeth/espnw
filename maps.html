About the Ritz Carlton at Dove Mountain

The Ritz-Carlton, Dove Mountain is an unforgettable Tucson luxury hotel set in the high Sonoran Desert. Located northwest of the city, in the scenic town of Marana, this breathtaking hotel features picturesque views of the Arizona desert combined with the majestic backdrop of the nearby Tortolita Mountains.
