Leslie Gittess

Leslie Gittess founded Blue Sky Media NYC in March 2010, after working in media, including professional sports leagues, for 16 years. At Blue Sky Media, Leslie helps early-stage companies with all aspects of their digital strategy. This work includes distribution and syndication of content, development of digital strategy and revenue opportunities, advertising and sponsorship sales and fund-raising.

Leslie worked for twelve years at the National Hockey League, as in-house counsel and then as a television and media executive. She then worked at MLB Advanced Media, Major League Baseball's award-winning digital division, as the Vice President, Business Development for four years. In this role, Leslie secured and negotiated agreements with third parties to license MLB content in interactive media, structured deals to acquire technology and services for the MLB.com portal and secured venture funding for MLB initiatives.

Leslie graduated from Columbia University with a degree in Economics and received a law degree from Georgetown University. Leslie is a former Division 1 athlete, having played tennis at Columbia University for four years. She is also on the Executive Committee of the UJA's Sports For Youth and Digital Media divisions, and the Alumni Board, Women's Leadership Council and Dean's Advisory Board of Columbia University.
