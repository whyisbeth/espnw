Erin Leyden

Producer, ESPN Films

Erin Leyden is a Producer with ESPN Films. She joined the group in 2008, and has produced over a dozen documentaries for the award-winning 30 for 30 series, working closely with filmmakers, including Barry Levinson (The Band That Wouldn’t Die), Steve Nash (Into the Wind) and Jalen Rose (The Fab Five), on development, production, and promotion of films. Leyden originally joined ESPN in 1998 as a production assistant on the SportsCentury: 50 Greatest Athletes series. Before joining the Films group she also worked at ESPN Classic and EOE Media Packaging, producing documentaries and specials, including Ali’s Dozen, The Greatest Game Ever Played, ESPN The Magazine at 10, and Her Story.
