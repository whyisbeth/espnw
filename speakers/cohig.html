Susan Cohig

Senior Vice President, Integrated Marketing
National Hockey League

Susan Cohig is the NHL’s Senior Vice President of Integrated Marketing, responsible for overseeing the management of all U.S. and select North American partnership marketing relationships, negotiating licensing of the use of League and Club properties, managing Club services and compliance matters, and regulating internal business units to maximize value. In this role, Cohig works to elevate League business and marketing activities through a continued partnership with our Clubs and support key League initiatives.

Most recently, Cohig served as the Senior Vice President of Club Consulting and Services. In this role, Cohig managed a department that worked closely with the NHL member Clubs on ticket sales, marketing and business operations initiatives in order to grow revenue and fan activation.

Ms. Cohig joined NHL Enterprises in November 1996 as Director of Advertising for NHL Interactive CyberEnterprises (NHL ICE) and in 1997 was named Director, Club Marketing. In 1998 she was promoted to Senior Director, Club Marketing. In August 2001 Ms. Cohig was promoted to Vice President, Strategic Development and in December 2005 was named Group Vice President, Club Services. Ms. Cohig was promoted to Senior Vice President of Club Consulting & Services in July 2007, before evolving into the new role of Senior Vice President of Integrated Marketing in July 2009.

Ms. Cohig began her business career at KYGO Radio in Denver Colorado, a division of Jefferson Pilot Communications, eventually overseeing all marketing and promotional activities for Jefferson Pilot’s Denver family of radio stations.

Before joining the National Hockey League, Ms. Cohig spent several years with Ascent Entertainment, overseeing the sales and servicing of corporate partner relationships of the NHL Colorado Avalanche and the NBA Denver Nuggets. While with Ascent, Ms. Cohig was a part of the management team responsible for the move of and introduction to the market of the Colorado Avalanche hockey team.

A graduate of the University of Denver (BSBA) and New York Law School, Cum Laude, Ms. Cohig lives in New York City.
