Ashley Koff RD

Celebrity Dietitian, Author, Health and Lifestyle Contributor

Ashley Koff is an internationally renowned registered dietitian who is on a mission to help people get healthy by bringing quality eating into every home. Koff's "Qualitarian" philosophy is founded on the principals of choosing ingredients that are organic, non-GMO, preservative and artificial ingredient-free. In order to help consumers easily identify these types of products she created The Ashley Koff Approved (AKA) list. A private practitioner, assistant faculty at the Continuum Center for Health and Healing at the Beth Israel Medical Center in New York, author of Mom Energy: A Simple Plan to Live Fully Charged and prominent lecturer, Koff regularly appears as a health and nutrition expert on national media outlets including Dr. Oz, The Early Show, Good Morning America, The Doctors, CNN and E!. Koff is also frequently featured in national publications like The New York Times, InStyle, Reader's Digest, O! The Oprah Magazine, Every Day with Rachael Ray, Redbook, Women's Health, Shape and is a contributing editor for Natural Health Magazine.
