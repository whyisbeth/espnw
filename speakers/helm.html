Maggie Helm

Sr. Director, Multimedia Sales, ESPN

Maggie Helm has served as Sr. Director, Multimedia Sales, in Burbank for all ESPN Networks, ESPN Digital Media, and ESPN The Magazine since 2011.

As a 15 year veteran in the Los Angeles advertising & media marketplace, Maggie began her career as a sales assistant at TBS and TNT. After climbing the ranks to Account Executive at Turner, she then moved to Disney in 2003 as an ABC Family Television Account Executive. Prior to ESPN, Maggie most recently headed up the Los Angeles ad sales office at Fuse, as their VP, Western Region.

Maggie received a bachelor’s degree in English/Creative Writing from Trinity College in Hartford, Conn (right down the road from ESPN’s headquarters in Bristol). She also played Division III Field Hockey and continues to be an active athlete, currently playing in two soccer leagues.
