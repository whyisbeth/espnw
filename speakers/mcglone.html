Julie McGlone

Coordinating Producer for the Creative Content Unit in ESPN's Production Department

Julie FitzPatrick McGlone is the Coordinating Producer for the Creative Content Unit in ESPN's Production Department. The CCU team provides high end creative content for marquee event and studio broadcasts. This includes show opens, teases, specialty branding campaigns, features and entertainment elements. Julie oversees a creative team of 15+ Producers, Directors, a writer and support personnel. Under Julie's oversight, her teams have produced creative content for some of ESPN's major broadcast priorities the 2010 FIFA World Cup, 2011 Women's World Cup, 2012 Euro Cup, ESPN's inaugural coverage of The Masters in 2007, Indy 500, Little League World Series and many more. Julie is currently working on the Sr. Management Team planning the 2014 FIFA World Cup in Brazil.

Prior to taking on a full time management role in 2006, Julie produced creative elements on many ESPN events including X Games, Winter X Games, Grand Slam tennis, Women's NCAA Tournament, NFL, College Football, and others.

Before joining ESPN in 2000, Julie worked as a Sports and Entertainment Promotion Producer in Detroit, MI, and as an Action Sports Producer in Vail, Colorado. In her 21 years in sports production, she has received 14 National Sports Emmy nominations resulting in 2 Emmy Awards for her work on the 2005 Winter X Games and the 2010 FIFA World Cup and various other awards.

A Detroit area native, Julie graduated from Oakland University with a Bachelor degree in Communications, and now lives in Avon, CT with her husband, Scott and their two young children, Tommy (3) and Katie (1).
