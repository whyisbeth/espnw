Eric Johnson

Executive Vice President, Multimedia Sales for ESPN Customer Marketing and Sales

Eric Johnson is the Executive Vice President, Multimedia Sales for ESPN Customer Marketing and Sales. Promoted into the position in October 2007, Johnson leads all domestic multimedia advertising sales efforts including all ESPN cable networks, ESPN Digital and Mobile properties, ESPN the Magazine, ESPN RISE, ESPN Audio, ESPN Deportes, BASS/Outdoors and ESPN Regional Television.

In 2008, min (Media Industry Newsletter) named Johnson's team as the "Multiplatform Sales Team of the Year." He was also named to the 2008 Mediaweek 50, a list of "the most indispensable executives shaping the future of media."

Johnson's tenure at ESPN began in 1999 where he served in National Advertising Television sales covering the Western Region. Beginning in 2003, Johnson assumed responsibilities as VP, National Ad Sales and served as Senior Vice President, Multimedia Sales overseeing offices in Burbank and San Francisco from 2006-2007.

Prior to joining ESPN, Johnson worked at TIME magazine in Los Angeles after spending a number of years on the advertising agency side of the business. Johnson served as a Media Director at Grey Advertising on Mitsubishi Motors and VP, AMD at Deutsch Advertising on the LA Cellular (now AT&T Wireless) and Baskin Robbins accounts. Additionally, Johnson worked at FCB and Rogge Effler & Partners on media accounts including, Mazda, Suzuki, Mattel, Sizzler, El Pollo Loco, Men's Health Magazine and Sunkist Growers.

Johnson was graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles in 1991 and received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics.
