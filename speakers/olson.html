Kathryn Olson

Chief Executive Officer of the Women's Sports Foundation

Kathryn Olson was named Chief Executive Officer of the Women's Sports Foundation in 2010, having served as a Board Trustee since 2005. She is the former SVP, Chief Marketing Officer of Shutterfly. Prior to Shutterfly, Ms. Olson was SVP, Chief Marketing Officer of LeapFrog Enterprises, a developer of technology-based learning products. She was in senior marketing roles for the Wm. Wrigley Jr. Company and Nordstrom, Inc., after a decade with The Quaker Oats Company, including several years with Gatorade Europe.

Ms. Olson previously served in board roles with Chicago Community in Schools, the Association of National Advertisers and the Women's Board of the Joffrey Ballet. She was named one of the Most Influential Women in Business in 2009 and 2010 by the San Francisco Business Times. She is an avid downhill skier and enjoys cycling, hiking and yoga. Olson has a BS from the University of Illinois and an MBA from the University of Chicago.
