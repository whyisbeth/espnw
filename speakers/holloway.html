Natara Holloway

Vice President of Retail Development in the Consumer Products department of the NFL

Natara Holloway is the Vice President of Retail Development in the Consumer Products department of the NFL. In this role, she is responsible for establishing the NFL’s retail growth strategy that focuses on elevating the NFL brand at retail and calendar events, enhancing the consumer’s retail experiences, and building sustainable retailer partnerships. Natara began working with retail as one of several new business initiatives in her previous role as the Vice President of Corporate Development – New Business Development. In that role, Natara worked to redefine the NFL’s event retail operations as a new growth business. Under this project, the NFL saw record increases in event retail revenue, launched an event retail rebranding plan, and expanded the presence of NFL and partner assets within retail executions. Additionally, she had a lead role in the development of the first ever, retail pop up store for women in New Orleans, and the team was recognized with a Commissioner’s Innovation Award as a part of the Consumer Product’s Women’s initiative.

Natara has been with the NFL for over eight years and has previously worked in the Internal Audit department and has also served as one of the Leagues Compliance Officers.
