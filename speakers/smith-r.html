Rose Smith

1980-1991 - Turner Broadcasting - Commerical Traffic, Cables Sales, Sports and Remote Field Operations. From 1991 - 1997 - VTE Mobile TV Productions - VP of Sales/Operations - Providing tv trucks and crew across the U.S. for Sports, Entertainment and Corporate Events. From 1997-2003 - Charter Broadcasting - National Accounts Mgr - Equipment Rentals and Purchases for ESPN, Turner Sports, Fox Sports, etc. In 2003, moved to XL Video as Director of Operations - XL Video providing LED Screens for Concert Tours, Corporate Events, Auto Shows etc. Left XL Video October 2011 to work with SOS Global Express. SOS Global Express is a National and International Logistics provider for the last 25+ years supplying shipping and planning services to primarily the Broadcast and A/V industry, such as ESPN, Fox Sports, Turner Sports, NBA, NFL Films, PGA, NBC, CBS for events of Football, Baseball, Soccer, Olympics, etc.
