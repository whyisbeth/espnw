Valerie Gordon

Coordinating Producer

Valerie Gordon is a Coordinating Producer of Features at ESPN, where she has worked since 2008. In her role, Valerie oversees editorial content and delivery of features for properties such as SportsCenter, NFL Countdown, NASCAR, "My Wish" and ESPN Radio's "The Sporting Life with Jeremy Schaap," among others. Prior to joining ESPN, Valerie spent 15 years in New York City as a feature producer at CBS' The Early Show and HBO's Real Sports with Bryant Gumbel, as well as on the broadcast of two Olympic Games (Atlanta 1996 and Nagano 1998). She is the proud mother of Noah, 9, and Julia, 6. In her spare time she enjoys Crossfit, running, hiking, and writing a monthly column about work/life balance. She most recently checked off another bucket list item when she completed a Tough Mudder and lived to tell about it.
