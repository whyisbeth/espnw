Kathleen Francis

President, Oasis Sports Ventures, LLC

Oasis Sports Ventures is a new start-up marketing and development company specializing in providing quality amateur sports programs and competitions for youth, young adults and families that encourages physical activity, social engagement and achievement. The first property to launch in 2012 is the National Family Tennis Championships.

Most recently, Kathy served as Managing Director, Marketing and Development responsible for implementing the USTA’s marketing initiatives to grow tennis participation and frequency of play, nationally.

Prior to the USTA, Francis served as SVP of Sales, Marketing and Communications at the NJSEA and was charged with attracting entertainment and generating revenues for Giants Stadium, Continental Airlines Arena, the Meadowlands and Monmouth Park Racetracks.

Francis also spent 11 years at Major League Baseball where she oversaw the League’s consumer marketing efforts. She created national advertising campaigns and yearlong platforms honoring the “All Century Team,” Jackie Robinson’s and Hank Aaron’s anniversaries. She reintroduced youth skills competitions, developed a four-on-four competition and oversaw the expansion of RBI - an inner cities program.

Honors include WISE Women of the Year in Sports Award, SBJ “Top 40 under 40” Sports Executives, 1999 and 2000 “Top 25 Women Executives in Sports”, “Ebony Outstanding Women Awards”, “2000 Boys & Girls Clubs of America Champion for Youth Award”.

In November 2011, Francis was named Chair and President of WISE (Women in Sports and Events), the leading voice and resource for professional women in the business of sports and events. A native of New Orleans, LA, she holds BS from Xavier University of Louisiana and a MBA in Marketing and General Management from Rutgers University.
