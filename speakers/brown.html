Lindsay E. Brown

Managing editor at Eco-Chick.com

Lindsay E. Brown is the managing editor at Eco-Chick.com, co-author of "Don't Hook Up," and interviewer of the acclaimed series "Heroines for the Planet" that's all about celebrating world-changing women. She's a contributing writer at LauraTurnerSeydel.com, Eco-Age.com, Ecover US and GatherGreen.com, and has written for the Edible publications and Cottages & Gardens. Lindsay is pursuing her M.S. in broadcast at Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism.
