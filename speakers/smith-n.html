Nichole Smith

The Walt Disney Company

Nichole Smith joined The Walt Disney Company in 2005 in the business affairs department of Disney/ABC Media Networks. She managed that group with respect to negotiating the various live-action and animation production deals for the company's children's cable television networks, primarily Disney Channel, Disney XD and Disney Junior.

In 2011, Nichole joined the corporate division and currently oversees the Corporate Synergy group for the company.

A native of Oakland California, Nichole has a B.A. from the University of Chicago and a J.D. from U.C.L.A. School of Law.
