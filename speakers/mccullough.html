Ashley McCullough

Chief Communications Officer for Velocio Events

Ashley McCullough is the Chief Communications Officer for Velocio Events. With a background as a professional cyclist, she has spent the past twelve years in marketing and branding with a strong focus on women’s and sports brands, including Athleta, Terry, Fuji, Brooks, Evo, Prana, and Victoria’s Secret. In her current position as the editorial director for the women’s sportswear brand Athleta, Ashley has been integral in the development of innovative multi-channel marketing strategies. She holds a BS in linguistics and economics from NYU and attended the MFA writing program at Columbia University.
