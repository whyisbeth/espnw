Nancy Hubbell

LEXUS PRESTIGE COMMUNICATIONS MANAGER

As the Lexus Prestige Communications Manager, Nancy Hubbell is responsible for public relations outreach to lifestyle media and developing Lexus’ philanthropy programs.

Through the “Lexus Pursuit of Potential” philanthropy initiative, Lexus supports organizations that help children realize their potential by building, shaping and improving children’s lives. Lexus and its dealers have committed up to $3 million per year, including the Lexus Eco Challenge, which debuted in September 2007.

Prior to her current position, Hubbell was Toyota National Strategic News Manager. She developed strategies and programs to promote Toyota’s environmental reputation in the media and provided public relations support to Toyota’s marketing efforts.

Some of the programs she has worked on include “Red Carpet – Green Cars” – what the stars drive to the Oscars, Toyota’s launch of the “Moving Forward” advertising tag line and Toyota’s bilingual Super Bowl commercial.

Hubbell has also served as National Manager for Lexus Public Relations, responsible for all of the division’s public relations activities including product launches, corporate public relations and auto show press events.

Hubbell joined Toyota in 1991 and has also worked as Toyota Media Relations Manager and Senior Administrator for Lexus Public Relations.

Before entering corporate public relations, Hubbell worked eight years at a sports and special events public relations agency on motorsports, boxing, football, baseball and other athletic accounts.

Hubbell lives with her husband, Tom Fournier, in Rolling Hills Estates, Calif., with their daughters, Gillian, 11, and Annalise, 7. When she’s not spending time with her family, Hubbell enjoys coaching softball, tennis, yoga and golfing. Her dream is to be a professional golfer, but she will settle for shooting in the 80s on occasion.
