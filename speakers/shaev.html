Hilary Shaev

Vice President, Marketing
WNBA, NBA Development League

As Vice President of Marketing for the WNBA and NBA Development League, Hilary Shaev oversees brand management, marketing strategy, and promotion of events and platforms for both leagues.

Shaev joined the NBA in March 2008 after a successful career in the music business. Most recently, she worked as Executive Vice President for Promotion at Capitol Music Group, Virgin Records, and Sony Music’s Epic Records. In these roles, Shaev developed and executed promotional campaigns nationally for all artists on each label’s roster, including Janet Jackson, Norah Jones, Lenny Kravitz, and Celine Dion.

Billboard Magazine named Shaev a Top Woman in Music in 2005, 2006, and 2007. A 1989 graduate of Duke University, Shaev resides in Westchester County with her husband Peter and their children Spencer and Audrey.
