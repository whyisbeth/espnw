Danielle Summers

Senior Business Operations Coordinator, ESPN

Recently promoted to Senior Business Operations Coordinator, Danielle will take on more complex projects as part of growing espnW’s relationship with ESPN Wide World of Sports. She will also continue to play a large role in the execution of the espnW: Women + Sports Summit. With seven years of ESPN under her belt, Danielle joined espnW in July of 2011. Ready to conduct business as Business Operations Coordinator, there is no part of espnW she does not touch. She is instrumental in ensuring the VP of espnW, Laura Gentile, is on schedule,responsible for keeping key stakeholders informed, and managing espnW financials.

Prior to espnW, Danielle worked for ESPN The Magazine in Business Development where she was the liaison between ESPN The Magazine and International properties (e.g. ESPN Brazil, ESPN Mexico and ESPN Deportes) managing content and photo credits. Earlier on in her freelance days, Danielle was responsible for tracking and analyzing expenses incurred by the photo department of ESPN The Magazine.

In her spare time, Danielle volunteers with her alumnae chapter, National Alumnae Association of Spelman College, The Junior League of Brooklyn and ESPN’s Voluntears. With the exception of ESPN’s Voluntears, she has held board positions with the organizations she has served.

What is the best part about keeping a team like espnW operating? She is having fun doing it all. It is this transition to espnW that lets her know she has finally found her niche in the epic world known as ESPN.
