Tina Thorton

Tina Thornton is a Senior Coordinating Producer in the Production department. She is responsible for overseeing the production of the NCAA Women’s Basketball Championship and 23 other NCAA Championships, including Wrestling, Lacrosse and the Women’s College World Series. Since March 2005, she’s also led Production’s Ad Sales Integration team, working with ESPN Sales to strategically develop sponsor opportunities across all of our studio shows and events. She has recently been charged with enhancing the career growth and development of our Producer personnel with over 150 people in this category.

Tina joined ESPN as a Production Assistant in 1993 directly out of college. From 1994-1998, she worked as an Associate Producer on a variety of events including ESPN’s Sunday Night Football, Big Ten College Football, College Basketball, the NFL Draft and several X Games and Winter X Games. Between 1998-2006, she served as Coordinating Producer on much of ESPN’s Outdoor programming including ESPN’s Great Outdoor Games, the Bassmaster Tour and the Bassmaster Classic.

Tina graduated from Wake Forest University in 1993 with a Bachelors of Arts degree in Communications. In 1999, she completed the Simmons College program for Developing Managers. She was also accepted into WICT’s (Women in Cable & Telecommunications) Betsy Magness Leadership Institute for the 2005-’06 year (Class XII). A yearly participant in ESPN’s formal mentoring program, Tina has served as a mentor since 2002 and was a recent member of the Mentoring Advisory Team. From 2002-2005, Tina served a 3-year term on the Wake Forest Alumni Council, representing nearly 40,000 alumni worldwide. And, for several years, she volunteered as a mentor in the Bristol school system and as a “Friendly Visitor”, matched up with an elderly person as a weekly companion.
