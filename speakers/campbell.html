Holly Campbell

Vice President, Marketing Communications for Enterprise Holdings

Holly Campbell leads strategic development and activation of all creative, media and sponsorship initiatives for the largest and most comprehensive car rental company in North America, which includes the Enterprise Rent-A-Car, National Car Rental and Alamo Car Rental brands. With annual revenues of $12.6 billion and more than 68,000 employees, Enterprise Holdings also owns and operates, through its regional subsidiaries, more than 1 million cars and trucks, making it the largest car rental company in the world measured by revenue, employees and fleet. Ranked No. 17 on the Forbes “Top 500 Private Companies in America” list, Enterprise Holdings is owned by the Taylor family and headquartered in St. Louis.

In this role, Campbell leads the creative development (television, print and digital), media planning and buying (television, print and online) and sponsorship activities for the Enterprise, National and Alamo brands. She is charged with stewarding the brands strategic positioning – maintaining separation and distinction in all consumer-facing touch points. She also is responsible for partner relationships including sponsorship properties, creative agencies, media agencies and networks.

Campbell joined Enterprise Holdings in 2005 after spending five years as Director of Communications for Anheuser-Busch where she managed internal and external communications for the company’s operations, crisis communications and labor relations. Prior to her tenure with AB, she worked for Fleishman-Hillard, a worldwide Public Relations, firm where her clients included Anheuser-Busch, Anheuser-Busch theme parks and Sony PlayStation.

Outside of her duties at Enterprise Holdings, Campbell is involved in community causes including serving as a board member on the St. Louis Sports Commission and for the Museum of Transportation and generously supports a number of social causes in the St. Louis area, including the United Way.

Holly is a graduate of the University of Missouri-Columbia with a bachelor’s degree in Journalism. She resides in St. Louis with her husband David and their daughter, Ella. She enjoys sports and traveling whenever possible.
