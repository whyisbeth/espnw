Kim McFadden

Vice President of Human Resources for the NFL

Kim McFadden is the Vice President of Human Resources for the NFL. Prior to joining the League in 2008, Kim worked for Johnson and Johnson Pharmaceutical R & D as Director of Human Resources, Motorola and Lockheed Martin.

Kim holds a B.S. Elementary Education from Elizabethtown College. She lives in Yardley, PA with her husband Robert and their two children Robbi and Shannon.
