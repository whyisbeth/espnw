Amanda DeCastro

Communications Manager

Amanda DeCastro recently joined ESPN as a Communications Manager where she manages public relations efforts for espnW and ESPN’s Emerging Technology group. Amanda also drives social media outreach for ESPN’s communications department. Prior to joining ESPN, Amanda was a Communications Manager at Time Warner Cable's Mid-Ohio Division where she managed internal and external communications, network events, employee recognition, social media, as well as marketing communication outreach to support TWC’s local programming linear and on demand channels and corporate philanthropic initiative, Connect A Million Minds.

Prior to joining Time Warner Cable, Amanda spent five years at Fahlgren Mortine Public Relations, one of the top 25 public relations agencies in the country, as a Senior Account Executive.

Amanda is recipient to several industry awards and recognitions based on her knowledge and commitment to the marketing and public relations industry – including Central Ohio PRSA’s Rising Star of Public Relations in 2009.
