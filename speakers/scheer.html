Heather Scheer

Heather Scheer currently works as a Director in the Business Affairs group. She started in 2008 as an Associate Director and over the past three and half years has been overseeing and negotiating all of the deals for ESPN’s films, including the 30 for 30 series and the upcoming Title IX series. She also works with the programming department on deals for soccer content, such as the EPL and UEFA.

Previously, Heather spent five years in MTV’s business and legal department, serving in a number of roles within MTV’s entertainment group. She joined MTV in 2003 where she was responsible for negotiating and helping to draft agreements for various reality and scripted shows as well as tent pole events (e.g., VMA's, TRL, Jackass, The Hills).

Heather graduated from Brooklyn Law School in 2008 as a Grant Richardson Scholar. She attended the part time night program at Brooklyn Law while working full time at MTV. Prior to that, she received a Bachelor of the Arts from the University of Michigan.

Heather has been asked to guest speak and mentor at many law schools in the New York area. In 2010 she was a guest speaker for the Sports Law JobTrack Program at New York Law School. In 2011 she was the recipient of Brooklyn Law's Sports and Entertainment Society award.
