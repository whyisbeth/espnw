Karen Brodkin

Executive Vice President, Business and Legal Affairs
FOX Sports Media Group

Karen Brodkin is responsible for overseeing all business affairs and related legal matters on behalf of Fox Sports Media Group and its wide array of multi-platform sports assets distributed through TV, digital, mobile, web, and broadband.

Since joining Fox Sports in 1998, Brodkin has been instrumental in completing hundreds of deals ranging from complex media rights acquisitions, to production, programming and talent agreements. She has overseen the contractual relationships with many of Fox Sports’ biggest professional and collegiate partners, including the NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, NASCAR, UFC, Big Ten, Big 12, Pac-12, ACC, and SEC.

Brodkin’s prominence in the sports media industry has been widely recognized by her peers and various trade publications. Sports Business Journal honored her on its list of influential women in sports business in October 2011; Multichannel News, named her one of the twelve influential “Women in the Game” in July 2011 and also one of twelve “Women to Watch” in cable television in January 2007; and Women in Sports and Events (WISE) recognized her as one of three annual recipients of the “Women of Inspiration” award in December 2011. In January 2012, Brodkin received the Association of Media and Entertainment Counsel’s (AMEC) Sports Counsel of the Year award.

Prior to joining Fox, Brodkin spent five years as an entertainment attorney and three years as a corporate attorney specializing in labor and employment matters.

Brodkin received a B.A. in Political Science/Art History from the University of California, Berkeley and a J.D. from University of California, Hastings College of the Law, graduating Order of the Coif.
