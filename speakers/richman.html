Katie Richman

Katie Richman leads the social media strategy and execution for the espnW team. In addition, she plays an active role on the ESPN-wide social media strategic team. During her 6+ years at ESPN, she has also worked in television production management for X Games and Winter X Games.

Prior to ESPN, Katie worked for both MTV Networks & Oxygen Media, in Brand Creative & On-Air Creative management. From working with the writers at TV Land to managing production shoots with Fabio, her time in Brand Creative created a passion for telling stories around brands and giving them voice.

Katie is a graduate of Providence College and a proud English major. She is a lover of all water sports: waterskiing, wakeboarding, bodyboarding, swimming...and suspects she may be part fish.
