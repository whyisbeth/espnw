Kirsten Atkinson

Media Director at Team One

Kirsten Atkinson is a Media Director at Team One, a division of Saatchi and Saatchi, where she heads up the National Broadcast, FEP Digital Extensions and OOH business for Lexus Automotive. New media is certainly a familiar area to her, having lead campaigns within the Social TV, Gaming, iTV, Branded Entertinament Addressable TV and Digital OOH spaces. Most recently, Atkinson has executed programs such as NBCU Shazam and Suits Recruits Social TV campaigns, First-to-Market ABC mobile and iPad launch partnerships, Award Winning 3-D Mapping Projection, MSN/Xbox Original Content Series and Zone Targeting for competitive blunting.

With over 13 years of experience, Atkinson is a forwarding thinking media professional with not only a solid traditional media background, but she is also well versed in the area of new media innovation. Having worked on an array of clients, from Miller Brewing Co, P&G, Symantec, Mitsubishi Automotive, Nissan North America and Lexus, her experience includes national and local planning across all media channels. Ultimately, Kirsten is responsible for producing the most innovative, first-to-market and ground breaking media solutions that will help push Lexus into new territory and connect the Brand to new audiences.

Specialties Include:
360 degree general market and diversity media planning, client, vendor and agency integration, budget management, client relations, innovative program development, broadcast upfront strategy development and implementation, buying agency management, product launch leadership and team building abilities
