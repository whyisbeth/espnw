Erin Kennedy

USA Rugby, Youth Development Manager

Erin Kennedy works for USA Rugby as the Youth Development Manager with a focused effort on growing more youth and high school rugby programs around the country. She have also taken an active role in girl's and women's rugby development within the organization. Erin grew up in Pennsylvania where she studied Clinical Psychology and Spanish at Marywood University. She moved to Colorado to pursue her Master's in Sport and Performance Psychology at the University of Denver. Upon graduation Erin worked part time for the United States Olympic Committee before obtaining a full time role with USA Rugby. Erin also represented Team USA as a Young Ambassador for the 2010 Youth Olympic Games in Singapore. She takes an active role in the rugby community as a player and a coach for girl's high school rugby. She is a Level 200 certified coach and Level 1 certified referee for USA Rugby. When she is not involved with rugby you can find her exploring the outdoors of Colorado and enjoying every moment with friends and family.
