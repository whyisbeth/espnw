Maureen Lindsey

Maureen Hassett-Lindsey is a talent executive with ESPN, where she works on the talent planning and development for all of ESPN's digital platforms such as espnW and ESPN.com. Maureen has been in her role with the talent office for about two years. Previously, she was a Coordinating Producer in the Sttudio Production department for over 10 years working on SportsCenter and ESPNews. Prior to working at ESPN, Maureen was the Director of Production at Lifetime Television for Women working on their sports properties. Prior to that, she was a Producer at the National Basketball Assocation for seven years where she worked on shows such as NBA Inside Stuff, NBC Halftime and Hoop It Up. Maureen has also worked for NBC on the 1996 Olympics. A three-time Sports Emmy winner, Maureen has been in television for over 25 years. She played basketball at the College of the Holy Cross where she received her Bachelor's degree in Sociology.
