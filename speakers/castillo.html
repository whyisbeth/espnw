Lori Castillo

Assistant Brand Manager, COVERGIRL

Lori graduated in June 2009 from The Ohio State University's Fisher College of Business with her MBA, concentrating in Strategy and Marketing. Upon graduation, she joined the Procter and Gamble family in their Beauty and Grooming Division working with the COVERGIRL Marketing Department in Baltimore, MD as an Assistant Brand Manager. She currently manages the COVERGIRL NA Delivery Portfolio, External Relations/Public Relation Strategy, Media and Communication Strategy, and the US Hispanic and African American Markets.

Prior to returning to Business school, Lori was employed with JP Morgan Chase in Chicago culminating a six year career which began at JPMC in Houston, Texas, as an analyst in the Leadership Development Program (a management training program). As well as serving as Chair for the Board of Directors for the National Hispanic Business Association from 2002 - 2007, Lori has been involved in her community as a keynote speaker and has spoken for groups such as the IRS, LULAC, the American Medical Association, and the Miller Brewing Company
