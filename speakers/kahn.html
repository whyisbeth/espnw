Britt Kahn

CONAN Talent Executive

Britt Kahn is a Talent Executive at CONAN, where she books athletes, politicians, and newsmakers for the TBS late-night talk show. She previously held the same position at The Tonight Show with Conan O'Brien. Britt began her television career booking breaking news guests for Larry King Live, Good Morning America, and The News with Brian Williams. She then transitioned to sports television, arranging athlete interviews for NBC Olympics and NBA on NBC. She later joined ESPN where she produced live segments exploring the intersection of sports with politics, news and entertainment, and produced taped features focusing on athletes' interests outside the game. Britt continues her long association with NBC Olympics, booking athletes for the network's coverage of every Olympics since Sydney in 2000. She has received four Sports Emmy Awards for her work on the Olympics. Britt holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in English Literature from the University of California, Santa Barbara.
