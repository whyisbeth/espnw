Kerin Hempel

Leads the Strategy & Planning department at New York Road Runners (NYRR)

NYRR is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the benefits of running for life. Widely known for producing world-class events—including the famed ING New York City Marathon—which attract and inspire more than 300,000 runners each year, NYRR is equally committed to youth and community services programs, which educate and motivate more than 100,000 children throughout New York City, in all 50 states, and around the world.

In her role at NYRR, Kerin serves multiple functions, including strategy formulation, business opportunity assessment, growth plan execution, and project implementation. Kerin joined NYRR in 2010 and helped to build the Strategy function within the organization. Prior to joining NYRR, Kerin was a management consultant at McKinsey & Company, where she advised senior executives on matters of corporate strategy, crisis management, business efficiencies, and organizational structure. At McKinsey, Kerin’s work focused on the sports, media, and financial service industries. Prior to McKinsey, Kerin worked at JP Morgan, on the institutional fixed income derivatives trading desk.

Kerin graduated summa cum laude from Princeton University and earned an MBA from Harvard Business School. She and her husband live in New York City with their two young daughters.
