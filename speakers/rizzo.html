Adriana Rizzo

Adriana joined ESPN in October 2008 and leads marketing for espnW, WatchESPN, Tennis, Golf, ESPN Insider premium content, and Grantland.

Previously, Adriana was the Vice President of Marketing and Product Management at Snac, Inc., a wireless startup in New York City that she helped found. Prior to Snac, she was Vice President of Marketing at Digicel USA, the MVNO US startup of the Caribbeanâ€™s #1 wireless provider. Adriana was responsible for building and launching the company. She created a new Brand identity and, partnering with the Sales team, secured distribution with key retail vendors such as WalMart and Best Buy.

Before Digicel, Adriana was Executive Director, Marketing at Verizon Communications responsible for marketing and product management. She led a cross-functional team that launched several new services converging communications, content and video. Her role required that she give frequent presentations, making her a sought-after speaker. She presented with Chairman Ivan Seidenberg at the Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas, at Verizon Board meetings and was honored to testify in front of the US Congress.

Adriana spent the first half of her career at several ad agencies, including Grey Advertising and The Martin Agency, where she led Newsweek Magazine, IBM, Compaq Computers, Sprint Cellular and AT&T.

Adriana was born in Argentina, grew up in Westchester and lives on the Upper West Side. She holds a BA, Magna cum Laude, in Spanish and English Literature from Harvard University and an MBA from Columbia University. She loves to travel, read, cook, run, write, take photos and live in NYC.
