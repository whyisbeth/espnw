Liesl Holtz

Global Women's Sports Marketing Manager at Oakley

Liesl Holtz is the Global Women's Sports Marketing Manager at Oakley. Over the last 10 years she has built an exclusive group of highly talented and accomplished athletes that endorse the brand and contribute to the growth of the women's active and sport category. With a small and focused women's team, Liesl and crew have developed brand identity and marketing around Oakley Women. Liesl is also Director of the Oakley Eco Coalition focusing on environmental impact of the overall Oakley business.
