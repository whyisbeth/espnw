Sue Rodin

Founded in 1996, Stars & Strategies provides strategic sports marketing for corporate clients such as the Danskin Triathlon Series, MassMutual Financial Group, ESPN, Conde Nast Publications, The New York Times, and many others. In addition, agency founder and president, Sue Rodin, is the exclusive marketing representative for ESPN basketball analyst Carolyn Peck, soccer Olympic gold medalist Carla Overbeck, among other elite female athletes and personalities.

Prior to launching her company, Rodin served as in-house consultant for Avon Products' 1996 Olympic Games sponsorship and licensing programs, for both domestic and global markets. Rodin was previously vice-president, director of promotion at National Media Group, Inc., a sports marketing agency in New York. During her six years at NMG, she created and managed national programs for blue chip clients and their sports affiliations. Earlier experience included senior positions at BBDO Promotion, Comart-KLP and Don Jagoda Associates.

Rodin is founder and chair emerita of Women in Sports and Events (WISE), a national networking organization for female executives. In 2009, The James H. Warsaw Sports Marketing Center at the University of Oregon named Rodin its Sports Business Woman of the Year at the annual Women in Sports Business Symposium. Rodin was appointed by Mayor Michael Bloomberg in 2003 to the New York City Sports Commission's Sports Development Corporation. In 2002, she received the National Association of Women's Business Owners (NAWBO) NYC Signature Award. In 1999, Street & Smith's Sports Business Journal ranked her #18 among the Top 25 Females Sports Executives. Rodin has chaired conferences for Strategic Research Institute and the American Marketing Association (AMA), moderated the sports panel at Harvard Business School's 12 annual "Dynamic Women in Business" conference, and participated in the AMA'S "Keynoters on Tour" program. Rodin has also served as a judge for the CableACE Awards. She is a frequent contributor to trade and consumer publications and has appeared on ESPN's "Outside The Lines," CNN, "NBC Nightly News," Fox Sports, "Rivera Live," and other media outlets. In 2011 Rodin was named to SportsBusiness Journal's inaugural "Game Changers"class.

A graduate of Ohio State University, Rodin earned a master's degree from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst.
