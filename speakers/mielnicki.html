Jenna Mielnicki

As Senior Director of Global Marketing Partnerships, Jenna Mielnicki creates and sells revenue-generating marketing partnerships for the NBA, WNBA, NBA Development League, and USA Basketball.

Prior to joining the NBA in 2008, Mielnicki spent four years as a marketing director at Teen Vogue, developing marketing and promotional programs to drive ad sales. She also spent time at The New Yorker and JCDecaux North America.

Mielnicki holds a bachelor of business administration with a concentration in finance from Emory University. She lives in New York City.
