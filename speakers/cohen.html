Cherie Cohen

Vice President Multimedia Sales, ESPN

Cherie Cohen, Vice President Multimedia Sales, leads a team responsible for west coast based national ad sales for all ESPN Networks, ESPN Digital Media, and ESPN The Magazine.

Cherie joined ESPN Customer Marketing and Sales as a TV Account Executive in NY in 2003. In 2005 she relocated to manage the Southern California sales office as a Sr. Director. She was promoted in October of 2007 to Vice President.

As a 16 year veteran of the media and advertising industry Cohen, a native New Yorker, began her career as an assistant media buyer at TeleVest, DMB&B NY. After holding several different positions at the agency she was recruited in 2000 by her client to relocate to Hollywood as a Director of National Broadcast in the marketing department at Paramount Pictures. In 2002 she found herself back in NY as a Television Account Executive for Court TV. Her bi-coastal client, agency, and sales experience have provided Cherie a strategic skill set and diverse network of relationships within the industry.

Cherie graduated Magna Cum Laude from Rutgers University receiving a BA in Communications and Psychology. Cherie, her husband David, and their two children reside in the Hollywood Hills.
