Alex Mallen

Sport Chalet

Alex Mallen began her career in sports while in college serving as an intern at numerous companies including IBSU.net Sports Broadcasting, Michael Epstein’s Sports Productions, Clif Bar Inc, and the Dallas Cowboys. With a resume built on internship experience, Mallen started her career as the Athletics Marketing Coordinator and Assistant Women’s Basketball Coach at California Lutheran University before accepting a position in March 2007 as a Marketing Assistant/Project Manager with the athletic footwear company, K-Swiss Inc. At K-Swiss, Mallen launched the first-ever social media platform while also managing the production of all trade catalogs. In August 2009, Mallen left K-Swiss and started as a Sales Representative with Nfinity Athletic Corp, a women’s performance footwear company. After just 5 months with Nfinity, Mallen was promoted to Regional Manager, overseeing the company’s entire Western Territory. In March of 2011, Mallen accepted a new challenge to grow the San Diego Territory as a Team Sales Representative for Sport Chalet, Inc. As of September 2012, Mallen had produced nearly 300% sales growth over 2011.

Mallen has served as the President of the Los Angeles Chapter of WISE since 2008. Under her leadership, the chapter has seen significant growth in membership, member resources and has attracted the support of top-level executives in the Los Angeles area. Mallen lead the creation and launch of the WISE Los Angeles Advisory Board and the chapter website in 2011. Mallen works continuously with the WISE National Board to create and renew WISE membership resources at the local and national levels and she is currently in the process of establishing a WISE Chapter in San Diego.

Mallen earned a 4-year academic scholarship and graduated summa cum laude from California Lutheran University in 2006 with a dual B.A. degree in Marketing Communication and Psychology. Mallen was selected as an All-Conference player as well as a 2006 NCAA "Woman of the Year" Candidate for her efforts as a four-year captain and starting point guard for the Cal Lutheran Women’s Basketball Team, who won back-to-back conference championships in her junior and senior seasons.

Mallen currently resides in San Diego, CA and enjoys running, playing basketball, tennis, golf, guitar, cycling, snowboarding, going to the beach and listening to live music.
