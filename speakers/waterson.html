Sheree Waterson

Chief Product Officer, of Lululemon Athletica Inc

Sheree Waterson serves as Chief Product Officer, of Lululemon Athletica Inc. Ms. Waterson served as an Executive Vice President and Chief Product Officer of Lululemon Athletica Inc. since June 2008. Ms. Waterson oversaw lululemon's Merchandise Management, Global Production, Community Legacies and Visual Merchandising. She has 25 years of consumer and retail industry experience. She served ... as President of Speedo North America, a Division of Warnaco Group Inc. since February 16, 2005. Ms. Waterson oversaw all aspects of Speedo's design, development, merchandising, marketing and sales. Ms.Waterson served as Vice President of Merchandising, Women's, for Levi Strauss & Co., where she spearheaded initial work on new women's speed-to-market and profitability models and pioneered the "fit logic" initiative. At Gymboree she served as Vice President of Merchandising and served as Chief Executive Officer of Enfashion.com. Her additional prior roles include senior level positions with Contempo Casuals and Wet Seal. She graduated from the University of California Berkley with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology.
