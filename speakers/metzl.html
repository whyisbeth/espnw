Jordan Metzl

Sports Medicine Physician

Jordan D Metzl, MD (www.DrJordanMetzl.com) is a nationally recognized sports medicine physician at Hospital for Special Surgery, America's premier orthopedic hospital located in New York City. In addition to his busy practice in New York City and in Greenwich, Connecticut, Dr. Metzl is an award winning author of two books, The Young Athlete and Sports Medicine in the Pediatric Office. He currently is the medical columnist for Triathlete Magazine. His next book, The Athlete's Book of Home Remedies, will be published by Rodale in 2012.

Dr. Metzl is often cited in news sources including the New York Times and Newsweek regarding the issues of health and fitness, and has worked as medical correspondent on both CBS and ABC national news. He has appeared on Good Morning America (ABC) and on the Today show (NBC) discussing the issues of fitness and injury prevention.

Dr. Metzl takes care ofathletic patients of all ages and lectures and teaches extensively both nationally and internationally. He has authored more than 20 medical articles and has been awarded two visiting professorships. In addition to his busy medical practice and academic interests, he practices what he preaches. He is a 28-time marathon runner and 9-time Ironman finisher.

Dr. Metzl is an espnW Advisory Panel Member.
