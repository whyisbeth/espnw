Jennifer Love

Coordinating Director, VP NFL Network

In June 2003, Jennifer Love was hired to help launch the NFL Network. She initially directed the NFL Network’s signature show, Total Access and her day-to-day responsibilities included handling the technical side of the live nightly show as well as all other shows in production. Love worked in the show control room and handled decision-making for cameras, tape playback, graphics, remotes and audio.

As the NFL Network has grown, Love’s primary responsibility is overseeing the on-air look of all NFL Network studio shows and Remote programming. Additionally, she oversees both staff and freelance Directing teams and Production Crews.

Prior to being hired by the NFL Network, Love was a mainstay at Fox Sports since shortly after the network entered the sports business in 1994, Love served as the network's Senior Associate Director for the Emmy-Award winning pregame shows. In all, Love has garnered five Emmy awards for her work as part of Fox NFL Sunday and Fox Saturday Baseball.

Living in California since 1988, Love is originally from Dallas, Texas. Love attended the University of Texas at Austin where she received a Bachelor of Science degree from the College of Communication. Love now resides in Playa del Rey, California.

Love is a member of the Director's Guild of America.
